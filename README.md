# CLESA - Skincare Ecommerce Store

Welcome to CLESA, your premium destination for skincare products. Browse and purchase from our curated collection of skincare essentials designed to enhance your beauty routine.

## About CLESA

CLESA offers a wide variety of skincare products including cleansers, moisturizers, and specialized treatments. Our platform makes it easy to discover and purchase the perfect products for your skincare needs.

## Features

- **Easy Product Browsing**: Explore our extensive collection of skincare products
- **User Profiles**: Create and manage your account with delivery information
- **Wishlist**: Save your favorite products for later

## Technologies Used

- **React**: Modern frontend framework
- **Node.js**: Backend runtime
- **Express**: Server framework
- **JWT**: Secure authentication
- **MongoDB**: Product and user database
